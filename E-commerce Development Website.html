How AR Works: The Core Principles

AR experiences are built on three fundamental pillars that work together to seamlessly blend the digital and physical worlds.

üìç

Tracking

The system must understand the real world. Using the device's camera, it identifies surfaces like floors and walls (markerless tracking) to correctly place and anchor virtual objects in the user's environment.

üé®

Rendering

Once the position is known, the 3D model of the product is drawn (rendered) onto the camera feed. This process makes the virtual object appear as if it's really there, reacting to the device's movement.

üëÜ

Interaction

Users can engage with the virtual objects. This includes moving, rotating, or scaling the product with simple touch gestures, creating a truly dynamic and personalized experience.
